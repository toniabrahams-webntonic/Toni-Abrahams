Homemade Brownie recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablesppoons of water
  • 1/2 powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup darl chocolate chips
  • 3/4 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. First, mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls.
  2. Use a bit of water with the eggs to achieve the moist, gooey texture needed.
  3. Make sure to sift the cocoa powder it if it’s lumpy
  4. Combine the sugar, flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, chocolate chips, and salt in a medium bowl.
  5. It's best to use olive oil as its gives off that rich flavour
  6. Then, whisk together the eggs, olive oil, and water in a large bowl.
  7. Next, combine the wet and dry ingredients in one bowl.
  8. 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla really enhances that chocolate taste
  9. Fold until just combined. Eventually the batter will be thick
  10. Then, pour the batter into an 8×8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper. Use a rubber spatula to spread it to all four sides of the pan and to smooth the top.
  11. Finally bake. Transfer the pan to a 325 degree oven and bake for 40-45 minutes.
  12. Test if properly baked with inserting a toothpick and if it comes out with a few crumbs attached it is just right.
  13. Allow the brownies to cool completely before slicing and serving.
